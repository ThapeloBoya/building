Who We Are

The Institute of Building Inspectors (IBI) is a registered NPC/NPO dedicated to advancing and professionalising building inspection in South Africa. We strengthen compliance, quality, and safety in the built environment through recognised qualifications, ethics, and CPD.

Vision: To be the recognised professional home of Building Inspectors in South Africa, ensuring every building is inspected to the highest standards of safety, quality, and sustainability.

Mission: Professionalise building inspection through standards, ethics, and qualifications. Partner with government, municipalities, and industry to strengthen compliance. Support inspectors with training, CPD, and career pathways. Advocate for inspectors’ role in safe and sustainable communities.

Our Partners

We advance careers through recognised pathways, uphold the highest ethical and professional standards, and drive advocacy both locally and globally to strengthen and support the industry.

Cost of Weaknesses

Weaknesses in the construction and building sector can have serious consequences, including lives lost due to building collapses and unsafe structures, significant financial losses from insurance claims, disputes, and failed developments, as well as delays caused by slow approvals and inefficient compliance systems. These issues ultimately reduce investor confidence in the sector, hindering growth and sustainability.

Key Programmes & Initiatives

The Institute of Building Inspectors offers a range of programmes and initiatives designed to strengthen the building inspection sector. These include the National Building Inspector Training Academy, the Inspectorate Professional Registration Programme, the Digital Compliance Partnership Project, the Youth in Inspection Programme which provides graduate placements, and the Annual State of Building Safety Report. Together, these initiatives promote professional development, industry standards, and the overall safety and compliance of buildings nationwide.
